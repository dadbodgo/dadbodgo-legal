Dadbod GO! – Account Deletion

We respect your privacy and make it easy to delete your account at any time.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Dadbod GO! app
  2. Go to the Settings page
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm by selecting Yes

Your account will be permanently deleted immediately after confirmation.

Data Deletion Details

  • Your profile information is permanently removed
  • Your saved restaurants, favorites, and activity are deleted
  • Your account cannot be recovered after deletion
  • Some limited data may be retained for legal, security, or fraud prevention purposes
