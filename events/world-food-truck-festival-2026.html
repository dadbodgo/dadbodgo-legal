Dadbod GO! Event Passport Official Rules

These rules apply to Event Passport giveaways hosted by Dadbod GO! unless otherwise stated for a specific event.

Current Event:
World Food Truck Festival
July 11, 2026
Downtown Chattanooga, Tennessee

Eligibility

  • Participants must be at least 18 years old or have permission from a parent or legal guardian.
  • An active Dadbod GO! subscription is required.
  • Employees of Dadbod GO!, event organizers, and their immediate family members are not eligible to win unless otherwise stated.
  • Void where prohibited by law.

How to Enter

  1. Purchase food from a participating vendor.
  2. Scan the vendor's official Event Passport QR code.
  3. Upload a photo of your purchase through the Dadbod GO! Event Passport.
  4. Successfully submitting the photo earns one (1) giveaway entry for that participating vendor.
  5. One entry per participating vendor. Visiting additional vendors earns additional entries.

Entry Period

Entries are accepted only during the official event hours unless otherwise announced.

Winner Selection

Eligible winners will be randomly selected after the event concludes. Winners will be contacted using the information associated with their Dadbod GO! account.

Photo Usage

By submitting a photo, you confirm that:

  • You took the photo or have permission to submit it.
  • The photo may be displayed inside the Dadbod GO! app.
  • Dadbod GO! may use submitted photos to promote future events and the application.

Disqualification

Dadbod GO! reserves the right to remove fraudulent, duplicate, offensive, or inappropriate submissions and to disqualify participants attempting to manipulate the giveaway.

General

Dadbod GO! reserves the right to modify, suspend, or cancel any giveaway when necessary due to technical issues, fraud, or circumstances beyond its control.

Privacy

Personal information is used only to administer the giveaway. Submitted food photos may appear publicly within the Event Passport experience.

Questions

Questions regarding these rules may be directed to Dadbod GO! through the official support channels listed within the application.
